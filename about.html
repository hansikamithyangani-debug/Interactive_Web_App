The Story Behind MediCare+

MediCare+ is Sri Lanka's first digital health platform focused on preventive care and follow-up care.

We're on a mission to transform the way Sri Lankans experience healthcare by putting prevention, personalization, and continuity at the heart of every consultation. We believe in care that doesn't stop at the prescription — we're here for the journey.

Our team of doctors and mental health therapists deliver trusted medical advice through text and video consultations, anytime, anywhere, making quality healthcare accessible to everyone.
