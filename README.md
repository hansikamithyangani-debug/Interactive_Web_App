MediCare+ is Sri Lanka's first digital health platform focused on preventive and follow-up care. Our mission is to transform healthcare by putting prevention, personalization, and continuity at the heart of every consultation. Theme: Modern, Trustworthy, Accessible Healthcare. 

Key Features

1. Digital Healthcare Solutions
 
E-Channelling: Book appointments with top specialists instantly.
Doctor Consultations: Video and text consultations with verified doctors.
Online Pharmacy: Genuine medicines delivered to your doorstep.
Emergency Ambulance: 24/7 emergency response service.
Laboratory Services: 1000+ diagnostic tests with home collection.

2. User-Friendly Interface
Clean, modern design with intuitive navigation.
Fully responsive - works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
Easy service discovery with search and filters.
Fast loading and smooth transitions.

3. Patient-Centric Features
Appointment Management: Book, view, and manage appointments.
24/7 Support: Multiple contact channels including hotline, email, and social media.
Home Services: Free sample collection and medicine delivery.
Health Records: Track your medical history (coming soon).

4. Interactive Elements
Service Carousel: Showcasing key healthcare services.
Location-based Services: Find nearby healthcare facilities.
Real-time Availability: Check doctor availability instantly.
Instant Booking Confirmation: Get immediate confirmation via SMS/email.
